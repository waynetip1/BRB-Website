Most exam prep is just questions in a list. PassBoard is built around six evidence-based learning techniques from cognitive science research — the same methods used by top medical and bar exam programs.

Retrieval Practice

Answering questions forces active recall — building stronger memory than rereading notes ever could.

Spaced Repetition

Your study schedule spaces sessions to catch information right before it fades — the most efficient use of study time.

Elaborative Interrogation

Every explanation answers "why" — because understanding beats memorizing 2-3× every time.

Pressley et al., 1992
