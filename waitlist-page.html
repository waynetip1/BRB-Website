What Happens in Class

Your 3-Hour Prep Session,
Step by Step

1

Arrival & Station Setup (30 min)

You arrive and set up your station exactly as you will on exam day. Your educator reviews your setup, gives feedback, and corrects anything that would cost you points with examiners.

2

Sanitation & Safety Protocol (30 min)

Sanitation is the single most-graded area on the practical exam. You'll walk through every step - what to sanitize, when, and in what order - until it's automatic.

3

Services Practice & Timing Drills (90 min)

You'll practice the required services - hair, nails, skin - with your educator watching, coaching, and timing you. Real feedback in real time. This is where confidence gets built.

4

Exam Day Mindset & Q&A (30 min)

Common mistakes, what to do if you lose your place, how to stay calm, what examiners actually care about. You'll leave knowing what to expect - not guessing.
