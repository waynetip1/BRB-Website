Our Story

We Know the Exam
From the Inside Out

The licensed Texas cosmetology educators behind Board Ready Beauty have spent over 35 years combined working behind the chair and in the classroom. Their careers span some of the most respected names in professional cosmetology education — including Paul Mitchell and Ogle School — with certifications from Sassoon, Pivot Point, and Wella Color that bring depth of knowledge most students never encounter in cosmetology school.

They've seen firsthand what separates students who pass the Texas state board exam from those who don't. And it almost never comes down to skill. It comes down to preparation. Knowing the format. Knowing what examiners grade. Knowing how to perform under pressure. Knowing what to bring and how to set up your station.

"Board Ready Beauty exists because talented graduates kept failing an exam that had nothing to do with their talent. They needed someone in their corner who knew that exam from the inside out — and who could show them exactly what passing looks like."

Board Ready Beauty was built to close that gap. The practical classes are small by design — because every student deserves real attention and real feedback, not a seat in a crowd. The focus is personal, hands-on, and built around one goal: getting your Texas cosmetology license.

Board Ready Beauty serves cosmetology graduates across the Dallas–Fort Worth area and is committed to helping every student who walks through the door walk out more confident, more prepared, and more ready than they've ever been.

What's Covered in the Practical Class

Every Section. Every Standard. Every Detail.

The practical class covers all 9 TDLR practical exam sections — so nothing catches you off guard on exam day. This is exactly what the examiners grade.

📋

Pre-Exam Setup

Station prep, draping, sanitation, and organization — scored before a single service begins. Getting this right sets the tone for the entire exam.

💅

Monomer & Polymer Over Tip

Proper acrylic application over a nail tip — product ratio, technique, safety protocol, and sanitation all scored by the examiner.

🩸

Blood Exposure Incident

Correct response procedure, documentation, and sanitation protocol. Most graduates aren't prepared for this one. We make sure you are.

🧖

Facial

Client draping, skin prep, product application, and massage technique — all performed to Texas state board standards.

🪒

Waxing

Application direction, removal technique, and post-wax care — safety and sanitation are heavily weighted in scoring.

✂️

Haircut

Sectioning, elevation, tension, and finishing — performed to the exact standards examiners score. You'll know what a passing cut looks like.

🌀

Perm

Rod selection, wrapping, solution application, processing, and neutralizing — every stage is graded.

💨

Blow Dry

Tool handling, sectioning, and timed finishing technique — performed efficiently without sacrificing quality.

🧪

Chemical (Relaxer)

Base application, relaxer application, timing, neutralizing shampoo, and conditioning — safety precautions are critical throughout.
