🛡️ The PassBoard Pass Guarantee

We built PassBoard to pass. If you do the work and still don't pass, we'll refund every dollar. Here's exactly how it works — no surprises, no fine print games.

Eligibility — all of the following must be true:

  • (a) You purchased PassBoard directly from boardreadybeauty.com
  • (b) You completed at least one full 100-question PassBoard practice exam within 7 days of your purchase date
  • (c) You sat for and did not pass the actual Texas PSI cosmetology written exam
  • (d) You submit your refund request within 30 days of your PSI exam attempt

How to request your refund:

Email support@boardreadybeauty.com with subject line "Pass Guarantee Refund Request." Include: (1) your full name and purchase email, (2) a screenshot of your completed PassBoard practice exam, (3) proof of your PSI exam attempt — your score report or a confirmation from PSI.

Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method. One refund per customer.

PassBoard Digital Product — General Refund Policy

PassBoard is a digital product with instant access upon purchase. Because of the nature of digital delivery, refunds outside the Pass Guarantee are not available — but if something isn't working the way it should, we want to know.

If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from accessing or using PassBoard, email support@boardreadybeauty.com and we'll get it sorted quickly.

Practical Exam Prep Class — Refund Policy

We understand that plans change. Here's how cancellations work for the $150 practical class.

If you need to cancel

  • Cancellations made 7+ days before the class date: full refund minus a $15 processing fee
  • Cancellations made 3–6 days before the class date: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 72 hours before the class: no refund. You may transfer your spot to another eligible cosmetology graduate for the same class date — just let us know.
  • No-shows receive no refund

If we need to cancel

If Board Ready Beauty cancels a class for any reason, every enrolled student receives a full refund or the option to transfer to the next available class date. Your choice.

Eligibility requirements

Students who arrive without a PSI-compliant practical exam bag and choose not to purchase the Board Ready Kit, or who are determined to be ineligible (e.g., have not completed cosmetology school), may be asked to leave. Refunds in those cases are at the discretion of Board Ready Beauty and subject to the cancellation terms above.
