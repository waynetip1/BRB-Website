🛡️ The PassBoard Pass Guarantee

We're confident PassBoard works. That's why we back it with our Pass Guarantee. Here's exactly how it works:

Eligibility Requirements — ALL must be met:

  • (a) You purchased PassBoard directly from boardreadybeauty.com
  • (b) You completed at least one full 100-question PassBoard practice exam
  • (c) You scored below 70% on your PassBoard practice exam
  • (d) You sat for and failed the actual Texas PSI cosmetology written exam
  • (e) You submitted your refund request within 7 calendar days of your original purchase date

How to Submit a Guarantee Request:

Email support@boardreadybeauty.com with subject line "Pass Guarantee Refund Request" and include: (1) your full name and purchase email, (2) screenshot of your PassBoard exam results, (3) proof of your PSI exam attempt (score report or confirmation).

Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method. One refund per customer, per lifetime.

PassBoard Digital Product — General Refund Policy

PassBoard is a digital product with instant access upon purchase. Due to the nature of instant digital delivery, refunds are only available through the Pass Guarantee program described above.

If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from accessing or using PassBoard, contact support@boardreadybeauty.com and we will work to resolve the issue promptly.

Practical Exam Prep Class — Refund Policy

Cancellation by Student

  • Cancellations made 7+ days before the class date: full refund minus a $15 processing fee
  • Cancellations made 3–6 days before the class date: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 72 hours before the class: no refund. You may transfer your spot to another eligible graduate (same class date, must meet enrollment requirements).
  • No-shows receive no refund

Cancellation by Board Ready Beauty

If Board Ready Beauty cancels a class for any reason, enrolled students receive a full refund or the option to transfer to the next available class date.

Class Requirements — Non-Compliance

Students who arrive without a practical exam bag and do not purchase the Board Ready Kit, or who arrive and are determined to be ineligible (e.g., have not completed cosmetology school), may be asked to leave. Refunds in such cases are at the discretion of Board Ready Beauty and subject to the cancellation terms above.
