Our Mission

Why Board Ready Beauty Exists

In FY2025, only 49% of Texas cosmetology operator candidates passed the written exam. That means more than 10,000 graduates - who put in 1,500 hours of school, who know how to do the work - failed because of how the exam tests knowledge, not because of what they know.

"School teaches you to do the work. The exam tests whether you can explain it. There's a difference - and most students find out the hard way."

Board Ready Beauty was built to close that gap. With over 35 years of combined cosmetology industry and teaching experience behind our curriculum, we created two tools that address both exams directly: hands-on practical prep classes covering all 9 TDLR sections (limited to 10 students for real attention) and PassBoard - AI-powered written exam prep with 600 practice questions, 30 focused topic exams, and real-time feedback engineered using proven learning science.

Our content is plain English, designed to be accessible whether you're a native English speaker or not. Our practical classes are small on purpose. Our tech is built to adapt to where you actually struggle - not where we assume you do.

The goal is simple: you finish school, you come to us, you pass your exam, and you start your career. That's it.
