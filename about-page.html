Why We Built This

Nearly half of Texas cosmetology students fail the written exam the first time. That number has never sat right with Teresa. These are talented, hardworking people who spent months in school learning their craft - and then walked into a test they were not prepared for.

The problem is not the students. The problem is that most exam prep tools are built for a generic national audience, not for the specific questions TDLR and PSI put in front of Texas students. PassBoard fixes that.

Every question is written against Texas Administrative Code Chapter 83 and Texas Occupations Code Chapter 1603. Every explanation is written to teach, not just to tell you the answer. Every feature is designed around how real students actually study - especially those who are working, raising families, and squeezing in study time wherever they can find it.
